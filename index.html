About

Syosset is a hamlet and census-designated place in Nassau County, New York, United States. Syosset is a welcoming city where you can spend time checking out the the local cuisine. Syosset might not have well-known attractions within the city, but you won't need to venture far to find top places to visit like NYCB Theatre at Westbury and Roosevelt Field Mall. Popular sights in the surrounding area include Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum.

Seasons

During the seasons, there are lots to do on Long Island! From exploring the beaches to visiting NYC. Syossset-Woodbury Park offers a children’s play area, picnic area with barbecue grills, basketball court 4 handball courts, shuffleboard courts, 5 tennis courts, swimming pool with diving area, kiddie wading pool (seasonal), 1 softball field, 1 Little League field, 1 multi-use turf field (baseball/softball, football/lacrosse/soccer), and nature trail.
